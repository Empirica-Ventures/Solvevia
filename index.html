The regulatory moment

SECP's mandate is already in effect.

SECP made IFRS S1 and S2 sustainability reporting mandatory in December 2024. Phase I companies report from July 2025, Phase II from 2026, Phase III from 2027. Assurance is required from Year 2.

In December 2025, SECP expanded its ESG Disclosure Guidelines to cover all PSX-listed companies and public interest organisations, aligned with the Pakistan Green Taxonomy. Boards must now form Sustainability Committees.

Every listed company and public interest organisation in Pakistan now has sustainability reporting obligations. Most are not prepared.
